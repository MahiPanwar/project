INTRODUCTION

Nasrullaganj, a tehsil of sehore district, is predominantly backward not only economic but also in social and cultural aspects. the main stay of the people of this region is agriculture. living under such a stressful conditions people could not afford to send their wards to cities for higher education. the college was openned in year 1986 with the aim to gratify the education needs of the huge socially and educationally backward population residing in this area. this college serves as an ambitious organization that motivates stusents of adjoining rural areas such as bawari, chakaldi, dimawar, gopalpur, ladkui and rehti to get enrolled there. with its meager resources, the college strives to shoulders immense responsibility.

The main purpose of establishing the college was to provide high quality education that would help the students to make their career.The college management introduces new job-oriented courses every year. Sports plays an important role in shaping up the personality and fitness of a student and give a truly global experience to all the students. Our college provides a platform to showcase their performance. government College nasrulaganj has well equipped laboratory with latest equipment. The college also helps the students to get benefits of government scholarships like “Gaon Ki Beti Yojna” and others to eligible students. The management is continuously trying to provide high quality education.

Nasrullaganj is situated among the picturesque and natural environment of Sehore District. It is situated 90 km from Bhopal, 55 km from Hoshangabad, 70 km from Harda and 72 km from District Sehore. It is the place enriched with natural beauty surrounded by many religious and tourist destination which attracts tourists. The famous religious Temple “Beejasan Mata” Salkanpur is situated 25 km from here. . Another famous religious place Neelkanth situated on the bank of Narmada is 7 km from Nasrullaganj. It has its own importance as it is also mentioned in Veda. Other religious destinations around Nasrullaganj are Gularpura, Ramgarha, Chhipaner and Vasudev.
